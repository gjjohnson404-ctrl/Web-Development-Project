About Me

A little more about who I am

Hello, I'm Grant

I'm a graduating senior at Karl G. Maeser Preparatory Academy with a 3.7 GPA and a strong commitment to service, leadership, and continuous growth.

My journey has taken me from stocking shelves and helping customers at Zurchers to leading youth groups as a camp counselor — experiences that taught me responsibility, patience, and the value of showing up with a good attitude.

Outside of work and school, I earned my Eagle Scout rank, which represents years of dedication to leadership, service, and personal character. I also received the President's Volunteer Service Award, a recognition I'm proud of because it reflects genuine care for the community.

I'm currently looking toward the next chapter — whether that's higher education, a new career opportunity, or both. I bring a work ethic shaped by real experience and a drive to keep growing.
