Dream Destinations

Every corner of the world holds a new story. Here are a few places that have stolen my heart — and might steal yours too.

Kyoto, Japan

Ancient temples, bamboo forests, and cherry blossoms — Kyoto is a living poem. Visit in spring for the full sakura experience.

Santorini, Greece

Iconic whitewashed walls, blazing sunsets over the caldera, and the freshest seafood you'll ever taste. Pure magic.

Patagonia, Argentina

Jagged peaks, glaciers, and absolute silence. If you want to feel truly small — in the best way — go to Patagonia.

Essential Travel Tips

  1. Pack smart, not heavy.
    • Roll clothes instead of folding to save space
    • Use packing cubes to stay organized
    • Pack a universal power adapter
  2. Stay connected without roaming fees.
    • Buy a local SIM card upon arrival
    • Download offline maps before you leave home
    • Use WhatsApp or Signal for international calls
  3. Eat like a local.
    • Skip tourist-trap restaurants near landmarks
    • Look for spots where locals actually eat
    • Try the street food — it's usually the best
  4. Keep your documents safe.
    • Store digital copies of your passport in the cloud
    • Use a money belt or hidden pouch in crowded areas
  5. Embrace spontaneity.
    • Leave gaps in your itinerary for unexpected adventures
    • Say yes to things that scare you a little
